Biography

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the ITML lab in the SIGS, Tsinghua University, where I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof.Shu-Tao Xia. I also work closely with Prof.Bin-Chen. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, in 2023. I enjoy playing basketball and doing some workouts in my spare time!

My research interests primarily include Trustworthy AI and Generative Models, with a particular focus on the reliability and robustness of Large Reasoning Models and Multi-Agent Systems.
